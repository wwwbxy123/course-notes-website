What is Project Management

project managers interested in growing their project management and leadership abilities should invest in emotional intelligence. Specifically they should develop their ability to use their emotions to facilitate thinking, understand emotional meanings, in empathy, and in their overall level of emotional intelligence. These were shown to be linked to the PM competencies of teamwork, managing conflict, and attentiveness

-- Emotional Intelligence for Project Managers, Anthony Mersino
